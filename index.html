Introduction
My name is Kyle Spencer. I am a 20 year old computer science junior studying at Penn State University. I hope to enter a career in computer science / programming.

I have been programming as a hobby for over 5 years and spend a lot of my free time on side projects. I like to experiment in any programming fields that I can find, including systems programming, full stack web development, machine learning, app development, and kernel development.

I am most skilled at web development in javascript with node.js, express, and angular. My biggest project in this area is my dynamic DVD catalog website that I was hired to make for my university's library.
